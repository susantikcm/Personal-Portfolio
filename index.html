Project list

These are practice projects created with Scratch Block Programming.

Flappy Bird

Descriptions...

Tap on the screen or press the Space key to control the bird to fly over all pipes without touching them. If the bird collides with any pipe, the game will be ended. Start playing choosing your favorite backgound.

Scary Shark

Descriptions...

Try to avoid the puffer fish and eat as many normal fish you can with the mouse pointer. Click the green flag in the top right corner first to play. As the shark gets bigger it gets harder to not touch the puffer fish.

Pacman

Descriptions...

Use the arrow key to move the pacman around teh enclosed maze to eat all yellow dots while avoiding the enemies that are chasing him.

Paint

Descriptions...

Use this as canvas to draw any object you could imagine with beautiful color. Kids could also use it to learn writing alphabets and numbers.

Ping Pong

Descriptions...

    To move the paddle to the left and right:
  • Player 1: use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle in red bar shape
  • Player 2: use the "a" and "d" keys move the paddle in blue bar shape

try to hit the ball to the opposite side. For every ball missed by the player, one point will be reduced.

Shoot the monster

Descriptions...

Move the space craft around the monster with the arrow key and press the Space key to shoot the monster, while avoiding being shoot by the monster at the same time. Both the player and the monter will lose point every time they get shot.
